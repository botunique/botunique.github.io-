About PixelDab

Art in my bones.
Design in my hands.

I'm a graphic designer specializing in brand identity and custom logos built around what you actually need. From T-shirts and posters to social media and murals, I work across print and paint to help businesses leave a lasting impression.

I love what I do — blending modern techniques with ideas from the past to make something that feels both fresh and timeless.
